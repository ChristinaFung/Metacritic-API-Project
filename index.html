How to Use_

"What Should I Play Next?!" is a simple video game recommendation generator that will return a top-rated video game based on your preferred gaming platform, game genre, and sleep levels. *wink wink*

Use this whenever you need to fill the void in your heart from finishing a previous game, or to confirm your awesomeness as a "hardcore gamer".

Have fun with it, and play ALL the games!
